## Anatomical Foundations
Endomidface works in a specific anatomical plane: the **subperiosteal space** of the midface. This is the virtual space between the periosteum (membrane covering the
bone) and the maxillary, zygomatic, and frontal bones.
### Why the Subperiosteal Plane?
- 
**Avascular plane:** Virtually no significant vessels between periosteum
and bone, minimizing bleeding
- 
**Anatomically defined:** Clear boundaries facilitate spatial orientation
during dissection
- 
**Nerve protection:** The facial nerve is superficial to this plane, protected
throughout dissection
- 
**Resistant to biostimulators:** Rarely affected by fibrosis from previous
treatments
