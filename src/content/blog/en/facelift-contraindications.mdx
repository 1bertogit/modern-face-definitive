## Absolute Contraindications
Situations where surgery **should not be performed**
under any circumstances until the condition is resolved.


## Relative Contraindications
Situations requiring **individualized assessment** and possible adjustments in
planning or preoperative preparation.


## Importance of Preoperative Evaluation
A thorough evaluation is necessary to determine if a patient is a good candidate for
advanced techniques like [Endomidface or Deep Neck. Safety always comes first.
> "The best surgeon is not the one who operates the most, but the one who correctly selects
> their patients. Refusing an unsuitable case is a sign of professional maturity."
> 
> - — Dr. Robério Brandão
A complete preoperative evaluation, including detailed medical history, laboratory tests,
and when indicated, multidisciplinary evaluation, is essential to identify
contraindications before surgery.
