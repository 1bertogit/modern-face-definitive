## Essential Qualifications
- • **Board certification** in Plastic Surgery
- • **Valid medical license** and credentials
- • **Documented experience** in facial surgery
- • **Own case photos** (not from internet)
## Questions to Ask at Consultation
- • How many facelifts do you perform per month/year?
- • Which technique do you use and why?
- • Can I see photos of cases similar to mine?
- • What are the specific risks in my case?
- • Where will the surgery be performed?
- • What is the postoperative care and follow-up?
## Warning Signs
- • Pressure to decide quickly
- • Unrealistic result promises
- • Price far below market
- • Doesn't show credentials or own photos
- • Very brief consultation without proper examination
