## Foods That Help
- • **Lean proteins** — chicken, fish, eggs (healing)
- • **Vitamin C-rich fruits** — orange, kiwi, strawberry (collagen)
- • **Green vegetables** — spinach, broccoli (vitamins and minerals)
- • **Pineapple** — anti-inflammatory bromelain
- • **Water** — abundant hydration
Proper nutrition is a key pillar of our [Modern Face philosophy for a fast and safe recovery. This approach, combined with following the recovery timeline strictly, ensures the best possible results.
## What to Avoid
- • **Excess salt** — increases retention and swelling
- • **Alcohol** — interferes with healing
- • **Very hard foods** — require intense chewing
- • **Excess caffeine** — can affect sleep and healing
- • **Processed foods** — inflammatory
