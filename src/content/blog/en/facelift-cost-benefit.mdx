## Value Beyond Money
Cost-benefit isn't just financial. Consider:
#### Time
Fillers require regular visits (4-6x/year). Facelift is single procedure with
lasting result.
#### Structural Result
Facelift corrects ptosis. Filler only camouflages. They're qualitatively different
results.
#### Self-esteem and Quality of Life
Hard to quantify, but impact on confidence and wellbeing can be significant.
#### Peace of Mind
Not worrying about "when will I need to do it again" has value.
