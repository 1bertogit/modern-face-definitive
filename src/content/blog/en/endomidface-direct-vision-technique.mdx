## How Direct Vision Endomidface Works
The technique works in the sub-SMAS areolar plane of the midface, using strategic access
that allows direct visualization of structures without need for large incisions.
### 1. Surgical Access
Temporal incision (within the hair) + access through lower eyelid when necessary.
Scars are completely hidden.
### 2. Dissection Plane
Dissection in sub-SMAS areolar plane (natural descent space). Different from Deep
Plane that enters musculature, Endomidface respects natural anatomical spaces.
### 3. Ligament Release
Controlled release of retaining ligaments (zygomatic-cutaneous, orbito-malar) under
direct vision, allowing vertical tissue mobilization without excessive tension.
