## Is Numbness Normal?
**Yes, it's completely normal.** Surgical dissection inevitably affects small sensory
nerves of the skin. This causes temporary numbness, tingling or altered sensitivity.
## Most Affected Areas
- • Pre-auricular region (front of ear)
- • Ear lobe
- • Lateral cervical region
- • Cheek (variable)
## Recovery Timeline
**1-4 weeks:**
Most intense numbness
**1-3 months:**
Sensitivity gradually returning
**3-6 months:**
Most recover normal sensitivity
**6-12 months:**
Complete (or near) recovery in almost all
