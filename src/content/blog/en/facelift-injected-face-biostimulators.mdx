## Why Do Biostimulators Complicate Surgery?
Biostimulators work by stimulating collagen production. This collagen creates **tissue fibrosis** ‚Äî scar tissue that permanently alters local anatomy.
### Poly-L-Lactic Acid (Sculptra)
Creates diffuse fibrosis in treated areas. Multiple sessions = cumulative fibrosis.
Peak fibrosis: 6-12 months
### Calcium Hydroxyapatite (Radiesse)
More localized but intense fibrosis. Microspheres may be palpable.
Peak fibrosis: 3-6 months
### Polycaprolactone (Ellans√©)

### Hyaluronic Acid
